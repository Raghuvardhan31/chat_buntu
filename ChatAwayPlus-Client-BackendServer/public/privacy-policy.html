Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2025

ChatAway+ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our messaging application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Required for account creation and verification via OTP
  • Profile Information: Name, profile picture, and status messages you choose to set
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers from your device contacts (with your permission)

Communication Data

  • Messages: Text messages you send and receive through ChatAway+
  • Message Metadata: Timestamps, delivery status, and read receipts
  • Contact Lists: Information about who you communicate with on ChatAway+

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, app version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, session duration, and interaction patterns
  • Push Notification Tokens: FCM tokens to deliver message notifications
  • Connection Data: IP address, network type, and connection quality metrics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Enable Messaging: Facilitate secure text communication between users
  • Account Management: Create and maintain your ChatAway+ account
  • Contact Discovery: Help you find friends and contacts who use ChatAway+
  • Message Delivery: Send push notifications for new messages and updates
  • Service Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance app performance
  • Security: Prevent spam, fraud, and unauthorized access to accounts
  • Support: Respond to your questions and technical issues

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Your Information With:

  • Message Recipients: People you choose to send messages to
  • Contacts: Your profile information with users in your contact list who also use ChatAway+
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that support app infrastructure (hosting, notifications, analytics)

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your messages with advertisers
  • Use your conversations for marketing purposes
  • Access or read your private messages except for security purposes

Legal Disclosure:

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect the safety and security of our users and services.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for message transmission
  • Secure token storage for authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Data encryption both in transit and at rest
  • Access controls limiting who can access user data

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information stored in ChatAway+
  • Update your profile, name, and status at any time
  • Delete specific messages or your entire account
  • Control who can message you and see your profile

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Message History: Messages are stored locally on your device and on our servers for message synchronization
  • Deleted Messages: Permanently removed from our servers within 30 days
  • Account Deletion: All associated data deleted within 90 days of account closure

7. App Permissions

ChatAway+ requests access to:

Device Contacts

  • Help you find friends who use the app
  • Enable easy messaging to people in your phonebook
  • Sync contact information for better user experience

Photo Gallery

  • Allow you to select and upload profile pictures
  • Enable photo sharing in conversations
  • Access your saved images for profile customization

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

8. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send:

  • New message notifications
  • Important account updates
  • Security alerts

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make material changes:

  • We'll notify you through the app
  • We'll update the "Last updated" date
  • Continued use of ChatAway+ constitutes acceptance of changes

10. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices:

Email: support@chatawayplus.com / Contact@chatawayplus.com
Response Time: Within 48 hours
